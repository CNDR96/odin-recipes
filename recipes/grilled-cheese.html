Grilled Cheese

Description

We all love a grilled cheese right? This timeless classic comes together in minutes and is great with a bowl of tomato soup, or just on it's own!

Ingredients

  1. Bread
  2. Butter or Mayo
  3. Cheddar Cheese
  4. Worcester Sauce (optional)

Steps

    Start heating a pan, and grate your cheese.
    Make your sandwich and spread either butter or mayo on the outside of the bread on both sides.
    Place your sandwich in the pan, until toasted to a golden brown and the cheese is melted.
    Enjoy!
