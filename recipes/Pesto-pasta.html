Pesto Pasta

Description

Enjoy an easy lunch with this simple pesto pasta.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Pesto
  • Black Pepper
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

    Boil a saucepan of heavily salted water and cook pasta according to instructions.
    Once cooked al dente drain your pasta, reserving some pasta water.
    Stir in your pesto, adding a splash of the reserved water if it needs thinning a little.
    Top with grated parmesan, a few grinds of black pepper, and enjoy!
